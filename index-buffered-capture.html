Welcome to the mapping tool

This app lets you style the map, freeze labels so they stay visible across zoom levels, highlight highways for route work, and export your project so you can reopen the same setup later.

Map Styling

Use the Land, Streets, Highways, and other panel sections to adjust colors and simplify the basemap for the look you want.

Freeze Items

Open Frozen Shields, Frozen Places, or Frozen POIs, then click map labels or symbols to keep them visible at all zoom levels.

Highway Highlighting

Search a highway ref in Highway Highlights to show live segments while zoomed in, then capture visible pieces so they still show when you zoom out.

Export Project

Use Export project to save your current map setup, styles, highlights, frozen items, and other saved work so you can load it back later.
